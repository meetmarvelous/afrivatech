About the Program

The iResearch Fellowship is fully funded and powered by AfriVatech. It offers a rigorous and immersive learning experience designed to bridge the gap between academic research and technological innovation.


Participants will gain hands-on experience in Proposal Writing, Data Analysis, Technology, and Innovation Development.
